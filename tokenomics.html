Tokenomics

PepePaws Coin (PPWS) is a token built on the Solana blockchain. The total supply of PPWS is 1,000,000,000 (1 billion) tokens.

Initial Distribution

  • Team and Advisors: 50,000,000 PPWS (5%)
    • Vested over 3 years to align with project goals.
  • Development Fund: 100,000,000 PPWS (10%)
    • Allocated to fund ongoing development, partnerships, and ecosystem growth.
  • Community and Marketing: 150,000,000 PPWS (15%)
    • Used for marketing initiatives, community engagement, and ecosystem development.
  • Staking Rewards: 200,000,000 PPWS (20%)
    • Stakers can earn rewards for participating in the network.
  • Liquidity Pool: 100,000,000 PPWS (10%)
    • Used to provide liquidity for trading pairs on decentralized exchanges.
  • Initial Exchange Offering (IEO): 200,000,000 PPWS (20%)
    • Allocated for sale on a reputable exchange to raise funds for the project.
  • Public Sale: 200,000,000 PPWS (20%)
    • Allocated for sale to the public at a fixed price or through a token sale platform.

Token Utility

  • Staking: Holders can stake their PPWS tokens to earn rewards and contribute to the security of the network.
  • Governance: Holders can participate in governance decisions for the PepePaws Coin ecosystem.
  • NFTs: PepePaws Coin holders may be able to use their tokens to purchase NFTs in the future.
  • Community Grants: A portion of the development fund will be allocated to community grants for projects that benefit the PepePaws Coin ecosystem.
  • Marketing: The community and marketing fund will be used for various marketing initiatives to promote the PepePaws Coin project.
